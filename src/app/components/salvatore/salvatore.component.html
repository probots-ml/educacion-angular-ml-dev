From Mis Ladrillos we intend to be a tool to develop the capacities of future generations. In addition to contributing to the change of the educational paradigm, expanding human development opportunities for future generations in Latin America. To be a benchmark for technological innovation in the field of education for the region. Our proposal considers all the necessary aspects for a comprehensive implementation.

The kit aims to work with the kids on the fundamental concepts of technology and robotics. The didactic proposals focus on the constructive, mechanical and logical aspects of robots, applying the new STEAM concept (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Mathematics) in the teaching of the different disciplines. In addition, a workshop classroom methodology is proposed that allows the development of fundamental skills for the 21st century, such as teamwork, the definition of roles and responsibilities, respect for the other, the capacity for experimentation, error as an element of learning, oral presentation and others.
